About

Summer Wollin is a full stack web developer specializing in JavaScript and the NEAP stack (Node, Express, Angular, and PostgreSQL). As a Certified Scrum Master, she values the Agile principles of individuals and interactions, working software, customer collaboration, and responding to change.

She has worked on numerous projects both individually and as part of a team. These projects are in production and can be viewed by following the links below or by visiting github.com/summerwollin.
